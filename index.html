The Story of Jesus

He was born in a humble place, in Bethlehem.

No palace. No army. Just a promise.

He grew up quietly, until at about thirty,

he began to teach about love, forgiveness, and God.

Crowds followed him.

The sick were healed. The broken were restored.

At his final meal, he spoke of sacrifice.

That same night, he was betrayed.

He was arrested, mocked, and crucified.

It seemed like the end.

But three days later, the tomb was empty.

Fear turned into courage.

His followers went out into the world,

sharing a message of love and hope.

This story changed history forever.

Amen.
