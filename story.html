耶稣传福音

Jesus Preaches the Gospel

耶稣走遍各城各乡，传讲天国的福音。
Jesus traveled through cities and villages preaching the Gospel of the Kingdom.

他医治病人，使瞎子看见。
He healed the sick and gave sight to the blind.

他告诉人们要彼此相爱。
He taught people to love one another.

耶稣说：
Jesus said:

“我就是道路、真理、生命。”
"I am the way, the truth, and the life."

“信我的人必得永生。”
"Whoever believes in Me shall have eternal life."

耶稣的爱直到永远。
Jesus' love lasts forever.

✝️ Follow Jesus 跟随耶稣
