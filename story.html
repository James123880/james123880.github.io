耶稣的故事 ✝️
Story of Jesus

耶稣出生在一个卑微的马槽中，但他的降生改变了世界。
Jesus was born in a humble manger, yet His birth changed the world.
他医治病人，使瞎子看见，使瘸子行走，向人们传讲神的爱。
He healed the sick, gave sight to the blind, made the lame walk, and preached God's love.
他告诉人们：神爱世人，每一个人都可以得到救赎。
He taught that God loves the world and that everyone can receive salvation.
然而，他被误解、被拒绝，最终被钉在十字架上。
Yet He was misunderstood, rejected, and eventually crucified on the cross.
他为世人的罪付上代价，用生命换来希望。
He paid the price for the sins of the world, giving hope through His life.
三天后，他从死里复活，战胜死亡。
Three days later, He rose from the dead, defeating death.
今天，他仍然爱你，等待你回应。
Today, He still loves you and is waiting for your response.
