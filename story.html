耶稣的故事 ✝️

耶稣出生在伯利恒，这是一个谦卑的开始。
Jesus was born in Bethlehem, a humble beginning.
他成长于拿撒勒，过着普通的生活。
He grew up in Nazareth and lived a simple life.
约30岁时，耶稣开始传道，教导爱与真理。
At about age 30, Jesus began to preach love and truth.
他医治病人，使瞎眼得看见。
He healed the sick and gave sight to the blind.
他教导人们彼此相爱，饶恕他人。
He taught people to love one another and forgive.
最终，耶稣被钉在十字架上，为世人的罪而死。
Eventually, Jesus was crucified for the sins of the world.
三天后，他复活，战胜死亡。
Three days later, He rose again and defeated death.
他的故事至今仍在改变世界。
His story continues to change the world today.
