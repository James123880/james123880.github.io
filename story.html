耶稣的故事 ✝️

耶稣出生在伯利恒，这是一个谦卑的开始。
Jesus was born in Bethlehem, a humble beginning.
他在拿撒勒长大，过着普通的生活。
He grew up in Nazareth and lived a simple life.
他开始传道，教导人们爱与宽恕。
He began to preach, teaching love and forgiveness.
他医治病人，帮助穷人，行了许多神迹。
He healed the sick, helped the poor, and performed miracles.
他为世人的罪被钉在十字架上。
He was crucified for the sins of the world.
三天后，他复活了，战胜了死亡。
Three days later, He rose again and defeated death.
他带来了爱、希望与永生。
He brought love, hope, and eternal life.
