耶稣出生在伯利恒，这是一个谦卑的开始。
Jesus was born in Bethlehem, a humble beginning.
他带来了爱、希望与光明，医治病人，安慰伤心的人。
He brought love, hope and light, healing the sick and comforting the broken.
他教导人们彼此相爱，饶恕别人，心存善良。
He taught people to love one another and forgive.
但他最终被钉在十字架上，为世人的罪献上自己。
He was crucified, giving His life for the sins of the world.
三天后，他从死里复活，战胜了死亡。
Three days later, He rose again, defeating death.
他的爱直到今天仍然存在。
His love still lives today.
