📖 Matthew Chapter 1
1 亚伯拉罕的后裔，大卫的子孙，耶稣基督的家谱：
1 This is the genealogy of Jesus the Messiah, the son of David, the son of Abraham.
2 亚伯拉罕生以撒；以撒生雅各；雅各生犹大和他的弟兄；
2 Abraham was the father of Isaac, Isaac the father of Jacob, Jacob the father of Judah and his brothers.
3 犹大从他玛氏生法勒斯和谢拉；法勒斯生希斯仑；希斯仑生亚兰；
3 Judah the father of Perez and Zerah, whose mother was Tamar...

18 耶稣基督降生的事记在下面：
18 This is how the birth of Jesus the Messiah came about:
19 他丈夫约瑟是个义人，不愿意明明地羞辱她。
19 Because Joseph her husband was faithful to the law...
20 正思念这事的时候，有主的使者向他梦中显现。
20 But after he had considered this, an angel of the Lord appeared to him in a dream...
21 她将要生一个儿子，你要给他起名叫耶稣。
21 She will give birth to a son, and you are to give him the name Jesus.
22 这一切的事成就是要应验主借先知所说的话。
22 All this took place to fulfill what the Lord had said through the prophet.
23 必有童女怀孕生子，人要称他的名为以马内利。
23 “The virgin will conceive and give birth to a son...”
24 约瑟醒了，就遵着主使者的吩咐把妻子娶过来。
24 When Joseph woke up, he did what the angel of the Lord had commanded him.
25 只是没有和她同房，等她生了儿子，就给他起名叫耶稣。
25 But he did not consummate their marriage until she gave birth to a son. And he gave him the name Jesus.
