He was born in a humble place, in Bethlehem. No palace. No army. Just a promise. He grew up quietly—until, at about thirty, he began to speak with authority about love, forgiveness, and the kingdom of God. Crowds followed him. The sick were healed. The broken were seen. At his final meal, he spoke of sacrifice. That same night, he was betrayed. He was tried, mocked, and crucified— a brutal end, or so it seemed. But three days later, the tomb was empty. Fear turned to courage. His followers went out to the world, sharing a message that still echoes today: Love, grace, and new life. This is the story of Jesus.
