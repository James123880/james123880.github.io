耶稣出生在伯利恒，一个 humble 的开始。
Jesus was born in Bethlehem, a humble beginning.
他医治病人，教导人爱与真理。
He healed the sick and taught love and truth.
他被钉在十字架上，为世人的罪牺牲。
He was crucified, sacrificing for the sins of the world.
三天后，他复活了。
Three days later, He rose again.
