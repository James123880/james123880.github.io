耶稣的故事 ✝️
Story of Jesus

耶稣出生在伯利恒，这是一个谦卑的开始。
Jesus was born in Bethlehem, a humble beginning.
他在拿撒勒长大，过着普通却顺服神的生活。
He grew up in Nazareth and lived a simple, obedient life.
三十岁左右，耶稣开始传道，宣讲天国的福音。
Around the age of thirty, Jesus began preaching the Kingdom of God.
他医治病人，使瞎子看见，使瘸子行走。
He healed the sick, gave sight to the blind, and made the lame walk.
他教导人爱人如己，饶恕他人，活出真理。
He taught people to love, forgive, and live in truth.
他被钉在十字架上，为世人的罪献上生命。
He was crucified on the cross, giving His life for the sins of the world.
三天后，耶稣从死里复活，战胜了死亡。
Three days later, He rose again, defeating death.
今天，祂仍然活着，赐给人新的生命与盼望。
Today, He is alive and offers new life and hope.
