耶稣的故事 ✝️
Story of Jesus
耶稣出生在伯利恒，这是一个谦卑的开始。
Jesus was born in Bethlehem, a humble beginning.
他在拿撒勒长大，过着普通的生活。
He grew up in Nazareth and lived a simple life.
他教导人爱与宽恕，带来希望。
He taught love and forgiveness, bringing hope.
他医治病人，帮助贫穷的人。
He healed the sick and helped the poor.
他为世人的罪被钉在十字架上。
He was crucified for the sins of the world.
三天后，他复活，战胜死亡。
Three days later, He rose again and defeated death.
祂带来了永恒的生命与盼望。
He brought eternal life and hope.
